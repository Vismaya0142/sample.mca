FICTION

Literature created from the imagination, not presented as fact, though it may be based on a true story or situation.

NON FICTION

Literary works that present factual information or narratives about real people, events, places, or ideas unlike fiction.

DRAMA

Books on drama encompass a wide range of literary works that explore the art, history and dramatic writing.

POETRY

Books on poetry encompass a rich array of literary works that explore the art, form, history, and interpretation.

SCIENCE FICTION

Science fiction books are a genre of speculative fiction that explores imaginative concepts involving futuristic science.

COMICS

Comic books are a unique form of visual storytelling that combines illustrations, text, and sequential art to tell narratives.
